VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement is entered into between ABC Corporation and XYZ Vendor.

1. SERVICES
The Vendor shall provide software development and maintenance services as agreed by both parties.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
ABC Corporation shall pay invoices within thirty days of receipt.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Vendor shall not disclose confidential information received from ABC Corporation to any third party.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement by providing thirty days written notice.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All intellectual property developed under this agreement shall belong exclusively to ABC Corporation.

6. LIABILITY
The Vendor shall be liable for damages resulting from negligence or breach of contract.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
