VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of 15 January 2025, by and between:

Client: LexLegis Private Limited, Bengaluru, India
Vendor: Alpha Compliance Services LLP, Mumbai, India

1. Scope of Services
The Vendor shall provide contract analytics support, document digitization, and compliance reporting services for the Client’s legal operations team.

2. Vendor Responsibilities
The Vendor shall:
- Deliver services in a professional and timely manner.
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations in India.
- Maintain accurate records of all services performed.
- Provide monthly status reports and incident summaries.
- Notify the Client within 24 hours of any material service disruption.
- Ensure that personnel assigned are properly trained and authorized.

3. Service Levels
- Turnaround time for standard requests: 2 business days.
- Critical requests: 8 business hours.
- Monthly service availability target: 99.5%.

4. Confidentiality
The Vendor shall keep all non-public information strictly confidential and shall not disclose, copy, or use such information except as necessary to perform obligations under this Agreement.

5. Data Protection
The Vendor shall implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal and confidential data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Fees and Payment
The Client shall pay INR 4,50,000 per quarter within 30 days of invoice receipt, subject to satisfactory performance.

7. Liability and Indemnity
The Vendor shall be liable for direct damages arising from breach of this Agreement, negligence, or willful misconduct.
The Vendor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Client from third-party claims, losses, and expenses arising out of Vendor’s breach, data breach, or legal non-compliance.

8. Term and Termination
This Agreement shall remain in force for 12 months unless terminated earlier.
Either party may terminate this Agreement:
- For convenience, with 30 days' prior written notice.
- For material breach, if not cured within 15 days of written notice.
Upon termination, the Vendor shall promptly return or securely destroy Client data.

9. Governing Law and Jurisdiction
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Courts at Bengaluru shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

10. Notices
All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and sent to the registered addresses of the parties.

Signed:
For LexLegis Private Limited
For Alpha Compliance Services LLP
