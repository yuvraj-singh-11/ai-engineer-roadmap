EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is made on 01 March 2025 between LexLegis Private Limited ("Employer") and Aarav Mehta ("Employee").

1. Role and Duties
The Employee is appointed as Legal Operations Analyst and shall perform assigned duties diligently, ethically, and in compliance with company policy.

2. Employee Obligations
The Employee shall:
- Protect confidential company and client information.
- Follow all information security and data handling policies.
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose potential conflicts.
- Complete assigned tasks within agreed timelines.
- Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Compensation
The Employee shall receive annual CTC of INR 12,00,000, subject to tax and statutory deductions.

4. Working Hours and Leave
Working hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday, subject to business needs and policy.

5. Confidentiality and IP
All work product created during employment belongs to the Employer.
Confidentiality obligations continue after employment ends.

6. Termination
Either party may terminate employment by giving 60 days’ written notice or salary in lieu, subject to applicable law and company policy.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by Indian law, and disputes shall be subject to courts in Bengaluru.
