CONTRACT TERMINATION AGREEMENT

This Agreement describes the conditions under which either party may terminate the contract.

1. Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate the Agreement at any time by providing written notice to the other party.

2. Termination for Cause
A party may terminate the Agreement immediately if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement.

3. Cure Period
The breaching party shall have fifteen (15) days to cure the breach after receiving written notice.

4. Effect of Termination
Upon termination, all outstanding obligations and payments become immediately due.

5. Survival
Confidentiality, indemnification, and dispute resolution clauses shall survive termination.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
