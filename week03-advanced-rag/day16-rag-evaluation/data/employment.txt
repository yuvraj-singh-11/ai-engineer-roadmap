EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between the Employer and the Employee.

1. Position
The Employee shall serve as a Software Engineer and perform duties assigned by the Employer.

2. Compensation
The Employee shall receive an annual salary of INR 1,200,000 payable in accordance with company payroll policies.

3. Working Hours
The Employee is expected to work 40 hours per week unless otherwise agreed.

4. Notice Period
Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days written notice to the other party.

5. Confidentiality
The Employee shall maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information obtained during employment.

6. Leave Policy
The Employee shall be entitled to annual leave in accordance with company policy.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
